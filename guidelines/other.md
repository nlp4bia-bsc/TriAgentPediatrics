# Routine Care & Other Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **Complex Cases**
  * Complex chronic patient with possible decompensation.
  * Abuse/violence requiring expert teams.
  * Fever with suspected imported tropical disease.

## **Class 3: PRIMARY CARE / NON-URGENT (7–10 DAY VISIT)**

* **Administrative & Follow-up**
  * Chronic disease control.
  * Post-discharge follow-up.
  * Vaccinations and routine check-ups.
  * Administrative consultations (referrals, diet requests, etc.).
* **General Symptoms**
  * Fatigue or generalized pain.
  * Isolated weight loss in >6 months.
