# Dermatology Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

*(Note: Severe burns/wounds listed under Trauma. Petechial rashes listed under Fever/Sepsis).*

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Infections**
  * Extensive skin infections.

## **Class 3: PRIMARY CARE / NON-URGENT (7–10 DAY VISIT)**

* **Minor Skin Conditions**
  * Small skin infections.
  * Minor cuts.
  * Mild rashes (blanching).
  * Acne, warts, cold sores, molluscum.
  * Hair loss, ingrown nails, diaper rash.
  * Skin changes (moles, small lumps, inflammation).
