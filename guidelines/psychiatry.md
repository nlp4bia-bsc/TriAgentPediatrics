# Psychiatric & Mental Health Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **High Risk**
  * Active suicidal ideation, recent attempts, or significant self-harm.
  * Aggressive behavior dangerous to self or others.
  * Acute psychosis with risk.
* **Severe Decompensation**
  * Inability to be safely managed at home.
  * Acute decompensation of a chronic disorder.
  * Severe eating disorder with life risk.
  * Substance use causing altered mental state or risky behavior.

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Moderate Distress**
  * Moderate anxiety or depression.
  * Suicidal ideation without a plan.
  * Low-risk self-harm.
  * Behavioral dysregulation.
* **Social/Chronic**
  * Eating disorders without severe complications.
  * School/social decline, bullying, isolation.
  * Problematic substance use with social consequences.
