# Toxicology & Environmental Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **High-Risk Ingestions**
  * Ingestion of batteries, magnets, sharp objects, or toxins.
  * Overdose (intentional or accidental).

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Low-Risk Ingestions**
  * Ingestion of non-dangerous objects requiring evaluation.
