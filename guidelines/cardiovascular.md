# Cardiovascular Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **Critical Signs**
  * Cardiorespiratory arrest.
  * Intense pallor with capillary refill >3 seconds.
  * Hypotension or shock.
  * Intense chest pain.
* **Rhythm Disturbances**
  * Extreme tachycardia not reversible.
  * Unstable bradycardia.

*(Note: Routine cardiac monitoring falls under General/Routine Care)*
