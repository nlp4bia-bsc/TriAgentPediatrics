# Ophthalmology Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **Vision-Threatening**
  * Eye pain or red eye accompanied by visual disturbances.

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Infections/Inflammation**
  * Painful red eye with swelling but normal vision.

## **Class 3: PRIMARY CARE / NON-URGENT (7–10 DAY VISIT)**

* **Minor Eye Conditions**
  * Conjunctivitis.
  * Red eye without swelling.
  * Itchy eyes.
