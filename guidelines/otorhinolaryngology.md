# Otorhinolaryngology (ENT) Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

*(Note: Severe airway issues like Stridor or Choking are listed under Respiratory).*

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Ear & Nose**
  * Otitis with discharge or intense pain after analgesia.
  * Ear or nose foreign body.
  * Nosebleed (epistaxis) without coagulopathy.
  * Dental/oral issues.

## **Class 3: PRIMARY CARE / NON-URGENT (7–10 DAY VISIT)**

* **Minor ENT**
  * Otitis, rhinitis, or pharyngitis without complications.
  * Mild sinusitis.
  * Sore throat, hoarseness, snoring.
  * Hearing issues, tinnitus, earwax removal.
  * Oral injuries, gum issues, halitosis.
