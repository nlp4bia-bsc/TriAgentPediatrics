# Allergy & Immunology Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: HOSPITAL EMERGENCY**

* **Anaphylaxis Signs**
  * Generalized urticaria or facial swelling accompanied by respiratory difficulty, vomiting, or dizziness.

## **Class 2: PRIMARY CARE / URGENT CARE (SAME-DAY VISIT)**

* **Cutaneous Only**
  * Generalized urticaria or swelling without other symptoms.

## **Class 3: PRIMARY CARE / NON-URGENT (7–10 DAY VISIT)**

* **Seasonal**
  * Seasonal allergies.
