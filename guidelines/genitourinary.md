# Genitourinary & Nephrological Triage Guidelines

## **Class 1: Urgent**

* **Urological Emergencies**
  * Acute testicular pain or suspected ovarian torsion.
  * Macroscopic hematuria (visible blood) without signs of UTI.
  * Proteinuria without signs of UTI.
  * Dark urine (brown/black/cola-colored).

## **Class 2: Moderate**

* **Infections & Sexual Health**
  * Urinary Tract Infections (UTIs) with or without fever but good general condition.
  * STI suspicion (without sexual abuse concern).

## **Class 3: Mild**

* **Routine GU Issues**
  * Genital itching, discomfort, or discharge.
  * Menstrual irregularities.
  * Incontinence.
  * Pubertal development concerns.
